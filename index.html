About ISKCON

ISKCON-Centre of Individual Resource Development International is a platform where teachers and educators are maneuvered to excel and mentor the student community through local and international mentorship.

ISKCON- offers opportunity to experiment and implement ideas, innovations, and collaboration to achieve performance and continuous professional excellence.

ISKCON- is where confluence of like-minded individuals share and imbibe knowledge, experience, through interaction, deliberation, and international exposure.
